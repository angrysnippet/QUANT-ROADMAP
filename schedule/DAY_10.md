## Block 3 — Quant Thinking (Generalisation)

**Problems:**
1. Toss 6 fair coins — P(exactly 3 heads)? Build it from counting and combinations.
2. Generalise — P(exactly k heads in n tosses). Derive it yourself.
3. Expected rolls until the first **1** on a fair die. You solved this for 6 — what changes? (Nothing — and understanding *why* is the point.)

**Hard puzzle:** With 50 people, which is more likely — (A) at least one shared birthday, or (B) no shared birthdays? Estimate, don't calculate.

**Career connection:** Generalising a specific result into a formula is the difference between solving one problem and solving a whole class of them.
