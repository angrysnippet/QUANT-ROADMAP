## Block 3 — Quant Thinking (Generalisation)

**Problems:**
1. Toss 8 coins — P(exactly 4 heads)?
2. Generalise — P(exactly k heads in n tosses). Write the formula yourself.
3. Expected tosses until **2 consecutive tails** — think in *states*, don't solve immediately.

**Hard puzzle (just think — we'll discuss later):** 100 prisoners, 100 boxes, each may open 50 boxes. Can survival probability be *significantly* better than random guessing?

**Career connection:** Spotting that 8-coins and n-coins are the *same* problem is the generalisation muscle quant interviews probe constantly.
